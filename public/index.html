What is it

Your team's workday, in one window

Priority Team Connect is the all-in-one desktop app for the Priority Property Acquisition team. It tracks work hours and activity automatically, keeps everyone talking through team chat and one-on-one messages, and lets you jump into a live call — with camera and full-screen screen sharing — in a single click. Schedule meetings, share files and voice notes, and stay notified even when the window is closed. It quietly updates itself, so the team is always on the latest version.

Everything it does

All the functions, in one app

Built for the day-to-day of a property acquisition team — nothing to juggle across five tools.

⏱️

Time Tracking

Start your shift and the app records your hours, activity level, the active application and periodic screenshots — automatically, in the background.

💬

Team Chat

Company channels for the whole team plus private one-on-one direct messages, synced live across everyone's app in real time.

📎

File Sharing

Send photos, PDFs and documents right in chat — images preview inline and files download in one click.

🎙️

Voice Messages New

Tap the mic, record a quick voice note and send it — it plays back inline in the conversation.

📞

Calls & Video

Start a voice or video call from any chat or channel with one click. A clear Answer button rings incoming calls until you pick up.

🖥️

Screen Sharing New

Share your screen in a dedicated, resizable call window you can maximize to any monitor — perfect for reviews and demos.

📅

Meetings

Schedule meetings on the team calendar, get desktop reminders, and join the room to talk in real time.

Notifications & Ringtones

Desktop notifications and sound for new messages, plus a ringtone for incoming calls — it keeps running in the system tray so you never miss one.

Roles & Oversight

Owners and admins see the whole team's day — hours, activity and screenshots — and manage user roles right from the app.

🔄

Auto-Updates & What's New

Themes

Getting started

Up and running in 3 steps

Install once — from then on the app updates itself automatically, no re-downloading.

Download the installer

Go to the download page and get PriorityTeamConnect-Setup.exe. It installs in seconds — no admin required.

Run it

Windows may warn once (it's unsigned): click More info → Run anyway. Future updates apply silently.

Create your account

Click Create an account, sign in, and check Keep me signed in. Your administrator assigns your role.
