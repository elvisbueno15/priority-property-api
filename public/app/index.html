How it works

While your shift is running, the app records your activity level (keyboard/mouse) every 30 seconds, the application in the foreground, and takes a screenshot every 10 minutes. Everything is stored on the team server.
You're using the web version — automatic activity, active-app and screenshot capture need the installed desktop app. Time still counts here.

Quick help

Time tracking: press Start in Time Tracking when your workday begins; Pause for breaks, Stop to finish. Your hours and activity are recorded automatically.

Meetings: you'll get a desktop notification when one is scheduled and 10 minutes before it starts. Click Join to enter the voice room and talk live.

Chat: use the team channels, or click a name under "Direct messages" for a private conversation.

Something broken? Use the form on the left — the owner reviews every report.
